
# Quivo Agency — Brand Assets

This repository contains the official brand assets of **Quivo Agency**, including (but not limited to) logos, icons, color palettes, typography, and visual guidelines.

These assets exist to ensure consistent and accurate representation of the Quivo brand.

---

## Allowed Use

You may use these assets only if you are:

- A partner collaborating with Quivo Agency
- A client referencing Quivo Agency in a factual and non-misleading way
- A press or media outlet covering Quivo Agency
- A community member sharing or promoting official Quivo content

Permitted uses include:
- Displaying the Quivo logo to reference the company
- Using assets in articles, presentations, or announcements that clearly refer to Quivo Agency
- Linking back to official Quivo Agency properties

---

## Prohibited Use

You may not:

- Modify, distort, recolor, crop, or animate the logo or brand assets without written permission
- Use the assets in a way that implies endorsement, partnership, or affiliation without explicit approval
- Use the assets for misleading, deceptive, defamatory, or illegal purposes
- Sell, sublicense, or redistribute the assets as standalone files
- Incorporate the assets into another logo, product, or brand identity

---

## Modifications and Derivative Works

Creating derivative works based on these assets is not permitted unless explicitly authorized in writing by Quivo Agency.
